# Business Plan

• Business: Standup — AI meeting-notes & action tracker
• Industry: Productivity
• Model: Per-seat SaaS
• Market: $1.9B TAM (High growth)
• Competitive advantage: Turns calls into assigned, tracked action items automatically
• Customer: Remote teams & managers
• Problem solved: Decisions made in meetings are forgotten and untracked
• Build investment (est.): $68,000 · operating cost $15,000/mo
• Projected profit (model): $18,500/mo

## Target Customer
Primary persona: Remote teams & managers.
Job to be done: remove the pain of "decisions made in meetings are forgotten and untracked".
Buying trigger: the cost/frustration of the status quo becomes unbearable.
Where they are: productivity communities, search, and peer referrals.

Phase 3: broaden the product line and open a partner API. Growth potential: High.
