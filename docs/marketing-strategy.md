# Marketing Strategy

Positioning: Turns calls into assigned, tracked action items automatically.
Channels: organic social + short-form video, content/SEO, targeted paid, and partnerships.
Primary metric: CAC vs LTV. North-star: customers who reach first value in week one.
Creative produced by Creative Studio.

## Content Calendar
Week 1: problem-awareness posts (the cost of "decisions made in meetings are forgotten and untracked")
Week 2: product reveal + demo video
Week 3: customer stories / social proof
Week 4: launch push + offer.
Cadence: 4–5 posts/week, 1 video/week.

## Email Campaign Plan
1) Welcome + brand story
2) The problem, framed
3) How Standup solves it
4) Social proof
5) Launch offer ($40/mo)
Then a weekly value newsletter.

## Sales Funnel
Awareness (content/ads) → Interest (landing page) → Consideration (free trial/demo) → Purchase ($40/mo) → Retention (onboarding + value emails) → Referral.
